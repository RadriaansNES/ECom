Setting and Manifesting Intentions

Mermagickal crystal creations focus on the power of intentions. What are intentions? Intentions are our soul’s deepest desires, or the dreams that we can make come true in our lives.

As powerful, magickal, Divine beings, we can choose to set and release our intentions into the universe. In doing so, they act as seeds of change in manifesting our desired reality (with inspired action and in Divine timing, of course). Think of it in terms of energy, as energy flows where intention goes. For example, if you’re looking to attract self-love, setting this intention signals to the universe that you are ready to receive it.

Each of Mermagickal’s crystal creations focuses on setting a specific intention. I do this by using a combination of intuitively chosen crystals, all of which have a specific connection to amplify the intention. I then program the intention into the crystals so that the crystals’ energy is focused. This ensures that the crystals are much more powerful and effective in cultivating the intention when they reach you.
