As powerful, magickal, Divine beings, we can choose to set and release our intentions into the universe. In doing so, they act as seeds of change in manifesting our desired reality (with inspired action and in Divine timing, of course). Think of it in terms of energy, as energy flows where intention goes. For example, if you’re looking to attract self-love, setting this intention signals to the universe that you are ready to receive it.

Each of Mermagickal’s crystal creations focuses on setting a specific intention.
