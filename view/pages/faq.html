What happens in a mediumship reading?

Evidential mediumship is a form of communication between a medium and an individual’s loved one(s) in Spirit. The intent of the reading is to allow the individual to re-connect to and feel the presence of their departed loved ones.

In a reading, information is received through Rachel’s intuitive senses and is delivered to the individual. This can be information about the loved one(s) in Spirit, such as their physical appearance or personality. It can also be information tied to the individual receiving the reading, or their family. An example could be a fond memory that was once shared. Healing messages are often brought through a reading, and signs and ways for the individual to maintain their spiritual connection to their loved ones after the reading.

What can I expect in an oracle card reading?

Oracle cards are a divination tool that have been used since ancient times. They are a powerful way to receive guidance from Spirit and receive clarity and perspective shifts about one’s life.

In a reading, Rachel intuitively chooses a deck of oracle cards to provide guidance to an individual. This guidance can be general or the individual can request that Rachel ask specific questions. Questions often revolve around themes like health, career, etc. Rachel pulls oracle cards from the deck, intuitively interpreting their words and imagery while channeling messages from Spirit.
