Mermission

Hi, I’m Rachel, owner of Mermagickal. When I ponder how best to introduce myself to you, the words that immediately come to mind are: Intuitive channel. Crystal and energy healer. Medium. Mystic. Oracle card reader. Spiritual teacher. But they cannot possibly capture the entire essence of who I am, a multifaceted being who deserves more than labels meant to fit me into a box. So instead, I’ll share a glimpse into the waters that led me here today and shaped who I am but, unlike labels, do not define me.

I have been diving into the realm of mermaids since childhood, which is not surprising considering how many lifetimes I have spent as one. I have navigated through the stormy seas of chronic pain, anxiety, and depression. I have been engulfed by the tidal waves of grief. And for as long as I can remember, I felt different. Different because I felt so deeply. Even if I blended in, no camouflage could prevent me from absorbing the emotions and energy of those surrounding me. Or prevent me from feeling less of my own.

I was seeking a way to flourish in a world that said I was too sensitive. Too emotional. Too much. Meanwhile, I had the most magickal team of Spirit Guides secretly steering me to do the opposite. To flow and remain soft while life cracks my heart wide open. To tend to the ocean of my emotions and energy while fully embracing the human experience. To embody my authenticity with the force of a waterfall. My Mum believes that these Spirit Guides are glistening here in this picture with me. I too feel that they were, shining their light as a message for me to do the same.

I first answered my Guides’ call, and the call of my soul, when I dove into the world of crystal healing. The magickal healing of these stones awakened me to the knowing that I have the ability to foster the joy and health I crave. I became focused on using gems to improve my health, which quickly led me to discover and incorporate Reiki energy healing into my practice. It was then that my health started to alchemize like a pearl in an oyster.

At the same time, the waters of my life were becoming salty, murky, and dark with the loss of my father. Through the ebbs and flows of grief, I swam to my new healing practice to keep me afloat. Somewhere along the shore, I found mediumship. Or mediumship found me. I believe that we always intended to meet, in order to help heal my grieving heart. Mediumship not only reunited me with my passed loved ones in Spirit, but nudged me to explore connections with many other magickal, multidimensional Spirit Guides.

In diving deep into the realms of Spirit and my soul, I realized that I was destined to develop my own channeling and mediumship abilities. After a few years of allowing my soul to guide me as I embarked on this journey, I blossomed into my truest self. I finally accepted my truth and decided to set aside the judgment and opinions of society. I came to unconditionally love myself, an intuitive empath who feels everything so deeply. A blessing and a curse, society says. But how can one not view this solely as a blessing?

For when we open the portal to our hearts, we open the gateway to our soul. We invite in the magick of our human experience, accompanied by the knowing that we are a soul in a physical body. We embrace health and happiness in every area of our lives. We step into our power and we cultivate our dreams. We own the beauty of loving fiercely and we radiate this love back into the collective. We begin to feel truly alive as we re-align into harmony with our mind, body, heart, and soul.

This is my mermission. Let’s face it, life on Earth is short. While we’re here, we all deserve to feel the utmost unconditional love and connection to ourselves, those on Earth, and those in Spirit. We deserve to heal as we bloom outwards from the infinite potential within us. We deserve to have the radiance of our unique soul shine. We deserve to sing our own unique siren song. And like a lighthouse, I’m here to help lead you into the light.
